Our Sweet Journey

Established in 2023, Khushi Food Products began with a passion for preserving the authentic taste of traditional Indian sweets. Our founder's family recipe for chikki has been passed down through generations, maintaining the perfect balance of flavors and textures.

We use only the highest quality nuts, jaggery, and other ingredients to create chikki that's not just a treat for your taste buds but also nutritious. Every piece is handcrafted with love and care in our small-scale facility.
